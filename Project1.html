The Distribution of Bombing Missions During the Vietnam War

Using ArcGIS Pro, Adobe Illustrator, Microsoft Excel

This simple points map shows about 2 million target points for bombing missions during the Vietnam War. I hope this map can give audiences a basic idea of the bombing campaign in the Vietnam War and shows how the Vietnam War also spread to Laos and Cambodia.

I changed the points' transparency to show the density rather than using ArcGIS's Points Density tool, so the pattern of distribution could retain a sharp and precise shape. I believe it's important for specific areas, such as the Ho Chi Minh Trail area. Finally, in terms of color matching, I used a darker color for the base map because I think it's a painful history, so I want to make the map less energetic by using grey and white hues.

This map is the second place winner of the 2022 GISCI Map Contest, Student Category.
