Santa Rosa Island Range Complex Site A-15

Santa Rosa County, Florida

According to the comment of USAF Major (Ret.) James V. Krasinski, this BOMARC A launch failure occurred on 19 March 1970, at Eglin AFB Site 15. Santa Rosa Island Range Complex is part of the Eglin AFB, its Site A-15 was widely used for surface-to-air missile testing and training.
