Nike Missile Site LA-43

San Pedro, California

The LA-43 Nike Missile Site was completed in 1956 and remained in operation until 1974. A group of volunteers from the Nike Historical Society tried to restorated the launch site to a museum in the beginning of this century, just like the SF-88. Unfortunately, the restoration efforts at LA-43 fell through and the museum will not be created.

Today, the LA-43 Launch Site is part of the White Point Nature Education Center and Preserve, and the LA-43 Control Site is part of the Battery Leary-Merriam. The control site buildings are using by the Marine Exchange of Southern California and the United States Coast Guard, so it's not open to the general public, but visitors could access the abolished radar towers outside the fence.
