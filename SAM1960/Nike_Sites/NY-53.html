1958 Middletown Nike Missile Explosion

Nike Launch Site NY-53

Middletown Township, New Jersey

On May 22, 1958, a Nike Ajax missile exploded and detonated seven other missiles lined up at U.S. Army NY-53 Launch Site in the Chapel Hill section of Middletown. Six servicemen and four civilians were killed, three more injured, scattered explosive warheads across a wide area of the countryside. The accident happened while soldiers and civilian ordnance personnel were installing new arming mechanisms. It is the most deadly accident involving the Nike missile system in history.
