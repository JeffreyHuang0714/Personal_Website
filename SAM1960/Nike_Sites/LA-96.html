Nike Control Site LA-96

Los Angeles, California

The LA-96 Nike Missile Site was completed in 1956 and remained in operation until 1968.

Today, the LA-96 Control Site is part of San Vicente Mountain Park, located at the peak of San Vicente Mountain. The site is maintained perfectly by the Mountains Recreation and Conservation Authority. Although the site has not been restored, there are still numerous self-guided interpretive displays about the site. The radar towers have been converted to observation decks, and visitors can access the site through multiple trails.
