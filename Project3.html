Glory of the Home Front: Military Production during World War II

Using ArcGIS Pro, ArcGIS StoryMaps, Microsoft Excel

This story map focuses on the importance of the home front during World War 2. The contribution of the home front is usually underestimated or ignored by the public. The story follows the Allies and Axis powers' home front to explain how the difference in production capacity impacts the result of wars. The story used dark colors as the theme to show the audience this story from a painful history. The story mainly used visual media such as maps, charts, pictures, and videos rather than words to tell the story because I believe graphics are easier and clearer to read by audiences.
